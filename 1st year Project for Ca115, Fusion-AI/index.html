    What's the big deal?

    High energy expenditures have an impact on everyday living expenses. Fewer money and savings. Everyone is affected especiall universities as they use a large amount on energy, and the severity will increase over time.Our objective is to build an AI-powered solution that will revolutionize the electrical system. We will collect data from free sources and analyze it in order to apply real-time solutions.

    Our solution is aimed at colleges or organizations with a huge quantity of office space or land, as these sorts of enterprises would profit the most from it. The savings generated in a normal family would be quite minimal, and while the product would still be useful, on a bigger scale, the product's actual worth and efficiency come through.

    How does it work

    Our database will collect exact information on current room attendance, weather reports, and energy efficiency in order to generate an accurate reading and scale of the energy requirements required for top performance at the lowest possible cost.

    Our device would be able to use technologies that were installed to address COVID-19 but are now obsolete, such as CO2 sensors. Our device may also make use of other systems and facilities, such as cameras, smart meters, movement-tracking lights, and so on.

    In the absence of the aforementioned resources, we could utilize alternatives strategies, such as hardware control panels if the institution desired master control. We might also use QR code scans, for example, at the start of each day/class to assess and anticipate the energy consumption required to get through the day at the lowest feasible cost.
